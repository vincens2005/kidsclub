Kid's Club Scedule:


First 5 minutes: homework #1
Next Song length: Playtime #1!
Next 2 minutes: Meeting (doing anything!) #1
Until end of homework: Homework #2
Next song-length: Playtime #2!
Next 2 minutes: Meeting #2
Until end: Free Time! (until you leave!)
